1. Open the TipCalculator app.
2. Enter the total amount in the box near Check Amount.
3. Enter the total number of people splitting the check in the box near the Party Size.
4. After entering all the number, press Compute Tip button.
5. All the results calculated will be displayed below the Compute Tip button.
6."Empty or incorrect value(s)!" message will be displayed if one or both boxes are empty while pressing the Compute Tip Button.
7. No negative number is allowed to enter in the box.
